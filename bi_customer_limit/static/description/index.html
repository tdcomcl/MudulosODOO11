Customer Credit Limit

With this module, you can set customer credit limit. You need to set a credit limit for any particular customer. So if any user crosses its credit limit, an auto-generated email will be sent to administrator along with that sale order reference. Another useful feature is, Customer can be put on hold. This feature is useful when customer crosses its credit limit with a lot margin. Once Customer is put on hold, customer will be unable to place any sale order.

Key Features:

  • Set Credit Limit for customers
  • Warning message to customer on crossing credit limit.
  • Auto-generated email to Administrator on cutomer crossing credit limit.
  • Disable customer to place sale order.

How to Use

In Customer, Invoicing tab you can set Credit limit, even put that customer on hold. Customer will be unable to place any order if put on hold.


While creating any sale order, customer can see its credit limit, also total amount that is receivable till now. Customer can place sale order even if total receivable is more than credit limit.


If customer's credit limit exceedes while creating sale order, when customer confirms orde. At that time a pop-up is provided that will show a warning to customer that credit limit has been exceeded along with exceeding amount and other details. On clicking confirm button, customer can confirm that sale order.


When customer's credit limit is exceeded, on confirming sale order, an auto-generated email will be sent to administration department. Mentioning, sale order name, customer name, amount exceeded and a link to review that particular sale order.


This is the sale order open by link provided to administrator via email.


If any customer exceeds its credit limit by a lot margin or it happens often times, you can put that customer on hold by selecting this option. Once customer is put on hold that customer will be unable to place any sale order.


While any customer is put on hold, if that customer tries to make any sale. After confirming order, a pop-up will be displayed to customer to contact administration department. Also that sale order will not be placed.
